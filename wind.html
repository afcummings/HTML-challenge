Wind vs Latitude

Wind speeds hover mostly around 5-10 mph regardless of latitude.

Higher wind speeds generally show up further from the equator on both the positive and negative ends of latitude. Based on this data there is not enough solid information to draw a conclusion about wind speed and latitude.
